Siete Foods Back to School Campaign

Campaign Strategy / Copywriting / Visual Advertising

Problem / Objective

The goal of this campaign was to create a back-to-school campaign promoting Siete’s snacks as a convenient and healthier option for busy college students. The campaign focused on single-serve bags that could easily fit into everyday student routines.

Target Audience

Health-conscious college students with busy schedules who need quick, on-the-go snack options.

Key Insight

College students want snacks that are both healthy and convenient. Back-to-school season is also a time when students are building new routines, so products that fit naturally into bags, planners, schedules, and daily habits feel more useful.

Big Idea

Wellness that keeps up with you.

My Role

I helped develop the campaign concept, connect the product to student routines, and shape the messaging so the brand felt relevant to college life.

Chris Collins Fragrance: Nonbinary Campaign

Brand Repositioning / Inclusive Messaging / Print Ad Design

Problem / Objective

Traditional fragrance advertisements are often heavily gendered. This campaign repositioned Chris Collins fragrance to appeal to a nonbinary audience through inclusive, identity-focused messaging.

Target Audience

Nonbinary and gender-nonconforming individuals ages 18–35 who value self-expression, identity, and inclusivity.

Key Insight

Consumers increasingly connect with brands that focus on identity instead of labels. Minimal visuals allow the audience to see themselves in the product, while inclusive branding builds a stronger emotional connection.

Big Idea

Not his. Not hers. Yours.

My Role

I worked on the concept, message direction, and overall positioning of the campaign to make the fragrance feel personal, expressive, and inclusive.

Wedding Website UX/UI Project

Web Design / UX/UI / User Experience

Problem / Objective

The goal of this project was to design a user-friendly wedding website that clearly communicated important information while creating a smooth and meaningful experience for guests.

Target Audience

Wedding guests who need quick access to details like the story, schedule, travel information, lodging, registry, and RSVP.

Key Insight

Simple navigation is critical for event websites. Guests should not have to search hard for important information. Storytelling also helps make the website feel personal instead of purely informational.

Big Idea

Simple design, meaningful moments, seamless experience.

My Role

I designed the website structure, organized the content, and focused on making the site easy to use, visually clean, and helpful for guests.

Alaska Airlines Senior Campaign

Advertising Campaign / Print Design / Audience Strategy

Problem / Objective

This campaign encouraged seniors to use Alaska Airlines discounted fares by reframing aging as a time of freedom, travel, and new experiences.

Target Audience

Adults ages 65 and older who enjoy traveling but are also price-conscious.

Key Insight

Seniors want to feel active, capable, and excited about life — not labeled as “old.” Travel represents freedom, connection, and opportunity, so positive messaging makes the discount feel empowering instead of limiting.

Big Idea

Golden years. Golden savings.

My Role

I helped develop the campaign idea and messaging approach, focusing on a tone that felt uplifting, respectful, and motivating for the audience.
