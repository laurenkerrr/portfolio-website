Portfolio Case Studies

A collection of campaign, branding, UX/UI, and creative advertising projects.

01

Siete Foods Back-to-School Campaign

Campaign Strategy / OOH / Magazine / Billboard

“Wellness that keeps up with you.”

Objective

Create a back-to-school campaign promoting Siete’s single-serve snacks as a healthy and convenient option for college students.

Audience

Health-conscious Gen Z college students with busy schedules.

Insight

Students want wellness products that fit naturally into their routines instead of interrupting them.

Creative Rationale

The campaign places Siete into everyday student moments like studying, commuting, and planning to make the brand feel connected to real college life.

02

Chris Collins Fragrance Campaign

Brand Repositioning / Inclusive Messaging

“Not his. Not hers. Yours.”

Objective

Reposition Chris Collins fragrance for a nonbinary audience through inclusive and identity-focused messaging.

Audience

Nonbinary and gender-nonconforming consumers ages 18–35.

Insight

Consumers connect more with identity and self-expression than traditional gender labels.

Creative Rationale

Rich visuals and minimal copy create a sophisticated and personal feeling while allowing the audience to define the fragrance for themselves.

03

Wedding Website UX/UI Project

UX/UI / Web Design / User Experience

“Simple design, meaningful moments, seamless experience.”

View Live Website

Objective

Design a wedding website that clearly communicates event details while creating a polished and welcoming user experience.

Audience

Wedding guests looking for quick access to event information.

Insight

Clear navigation and organized information are essential for event websites.

Creative Rationale

The design uses soft colors, card layouts, and clean spacing to make the website feel personal while remaining functional.

04

Alaska Airlines Senior Campaign

Print Advertising / Campaign Messaging

“Golden years. Golden savings.”

Objective

Encourage seniors to use Alaska Airlines discounted fares by reframing aging as freedom and opportunity.

Audience

Adults 65+ who enjoy travel but are still budget-conscious.

Insight

Seniors want to feel active and independent, not defined by age.

Creative Rationale

Positive messaging and warm visuals make the discount feel empowering instead of limiting.
