01

Siete Foods Back-to-School Campaign

Campaign Strategy / OOH / Magazine / Billboard

“Wellness that keeps up with you.”

Objective

Create a back-to-school campaign that promotes Siete single-serve snack bags as a convenient and better-for-you option for busy college students.

Audience

Gen Z wellness seekers and health-conscious college students who want snacks that are quick, convenient, and easy to bring with them.

Insight

Students are balancing classes, studying, commuting, and social life. They want wellness products that fit into their routine instead of interrupting it.

Creative Rationale

The campaign places Siete in everyday student moments like backpacks, planners, studying, and commuting. The bright colors and short headlines make the work feel energetic, readable, and connected to college life.

02

Chris Collins Fragrance Nonbinary Campaign

Brand Repositioning / Inclusive Messaging / Print / Digital

“Not his. Not hers. Yours.”

Objective

Reposition Chris Collins fragrance for a nonbinary audience by moving away from traditional gendered fragrance advertising.

Audience

Nonbinary and gender-nonconforming individuals ages 18–35 who value self-expression, inclusivity, and identity.

Insight

Fragrance is personal. Consumers want brands that let them define themselves instead of being placed into labels like masculine or feminine.

Creative Rationale

The campaign uses rich, earthy visuals and minimal copy to make the fragrance feel sophisticated, personal, and open to interpretation. The line “Not his. Not hers. Yours.” makes the message direct and inclusive.

03

Wedding Website UX/UI Project

UX/UI / Web Design / User Experience

“Simple design, meaningful moments, seamless experience.”

View Live Website

Objective

Design a wedding website that clearly communicates important event information while creating a smooth and welcoming experience for guests.

Audience

Wedding guests who need quick access to the story, RSVP, registry, ceremony details, reception details, lodging, and travel information.

Insight

Guests should not have to search for basic information. Clear navigation, organized sections, and simple design help make the site useful and easy to understand.

Creative Rationale

I used a clean layout, soft colors, card sections, and a clear information hierarchy to make the website feel personal but still functional.

04

Alaska Airlines Senior Campaign

Print Advertising / Audience Strategy / Campaign Messaging

“Golden years. Golden savings.”

Objective

Encourage seniors to use Alaska Airlines discounted fares by reframing aging as a time of freedom, travel, and connection.

Audience

Adults ages 65 and older who enjoy travel but are also price-conscious.

Insight

Seniors want to feel active and independent, not defined by age. Travel represents freedom and new experiences.

Creative Rationale

The campaign uses positive language and warm visuals to make the discount feel empowering, exciting, and aspirational.
